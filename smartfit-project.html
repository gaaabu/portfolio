<h2>SmartFit Fitness App</h2>

Kotlin

Smartfit Fitness App is a comprehensive mobile application built to modernize the traditional gym experience. It eliminates the need for pen-and-paper, or memorization logging by offering a seamless digital interface for tracking progressive overload and routine management.

  • Precision Tracking: A built-in logger allows users to record sets, reps, and weights in real-time to ensure consistent progress.
  • Flexible Routine Management: Users can design their own workout splits from scratch or import pre-set routines used by famous bodybuilders.
  • Integrated AI Assistant: An embedded AI chatbot provides instant answers to users' questions regarding form, nutrition, and recovery, acting as a 24/7 personal trainer.
