<h2>STOCK</h2>

PHP HTML CSS SQL JavaScript Java

A full-stack inventory management solution designed to bridge the gap between physical warehouses and digital records. The system consists of a web-based dashboard for managing stock batches and generating QR labels, paired with a native Android application that scans these codes to trigger imeediate stock updates. This ecosystem eliminates manual entry errors by enforcing a "scan-to-verify" workflow, ensuring that the physical item matches the digital record before any transaction is finalized.

View Demo!

QR Code Scanner App Connected to the Website

A dedicated scanner app connected to the system. Scanning a physical product's QR code instantly navigates to the specific "Product Out" page for that batch, eliminating manual searching and data entry errors.

Batch-Level Inventory

Tracks individual batches of products rather than just total quantities, allowing for granular control over stock.

Audit Trails

A detailed "Records" log of every stock-in and stock-out transaction, categorized by date and action type.

Safety Workflow

A "Confirmation Page" where pending stock-out actions are reviewed. Users can verify qunatities or "Undo" actions before they are permanently committed to the records.
