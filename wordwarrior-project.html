<h2>Word Warrior</h2>

Scratch Adobe Premiere Pro Adobe After Effects

In this game, opponents throw words at the player, who must type them quickly and accurately to deal damage or defeat enemies. If an enemy-generated word reaches the player, their health decreases, potentially leading to a game over if fully depleted. The game includes a single power-up, Rage Mode, which ddcocubles Faris' damage. Correctly using this power-up is crucial in key situations, enabling him to swiftly eleminate foes and turn the tide of battle in his favor.
